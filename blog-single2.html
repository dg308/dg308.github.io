The Rise of Vegan Eating and Benefits of Gluten-Free Foods

  • Devanshu Goyal
  • Health & Nutrition
  • 5th July 2023

The global shift towards veganism and plant-based diets has been monumental in promoting health and sustainability. With the increasing awareness of the benefits of such diets, gluten-free foods like millet are gaining popularity, especially in the West.

Millet, a gluten-free grain, is not just versatile in culinary applications but is also packed with nutrients. Rich in fiber, proteins, and essential minerals, it's a boon for those with gluten sensitivities or celiac disease.

The Western world is now realizing the potential of ancient grains like millet, which have been staples in other parts of the world for centuries. Their introduction into mainstream diets can be a game-changer in addressing various health issues related to gluten consumption.

Moreover, the environmental benefits of veganism and the consumption of sustainable crops like millet cannot be overstated. They require less water, are drought-resistant, and can be grown without the heavy use of pesticides, making them eco-friendly choices.

In conclusion, as the world becomes more health-conscious and environmentally aware, the shift towards vegan diets and the inclusion of gluten-free foods is not just a trend but a necessity. Embracing these dietary choices can lead to a healthier population and a greener planet.
